Why study plants?
Why should we care?

You might think that plants are boring because they are immobile and silent.
But guess what, plants are the world best chemists! They make lots of compounds we cannot make ourselves.
When talking about life on land, plants are at the base of everything (primary producers).
They make food and provide shelter for not only humans but all terrestrial organisms.
Yet, we know very little about how they work from molecular point of view.
I believe understanding plants is essential in understanding everything else that rely on them.
