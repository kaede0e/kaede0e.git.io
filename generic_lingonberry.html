Lingonberry genomics:
Genome assembly and phylogenetics

Being nutritious and tasty, lingonberry has been cherished by local communities and wildlife for a long time.
While its phytochemical properties are well established,
not so much is known about its ecology, evolutionary history, or genomic diversity.
We aim to provide genomic resource for this important potential crop species for the future breeding or biodiversity survey efforts.
